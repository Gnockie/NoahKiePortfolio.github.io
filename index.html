About me

Hello! I'm Noah Kienesberger, a 18-year-old student at HTL Spengergasse, where I'm learning about the world of Game Design. Ever since I can remember, I've been captivated by the power of digital art and the limitless possibilities of creating games.

My Journey

From a young age, I was fascinated by video games and the artistry behind them. Starting with the timeless game Minecraft, this passion led me to HTL Spengergasse, a school known for its focus on technical and creative education.

What I love

  • Game Design: Creating engaging and innovative games to entertain players.

  • Digital Art: Bringing ideas to life through digital painting, 3D modeling, and animation.

  • Storytelling: Crafting compelling narratives that make games more than just a series of levels but an experience, like in the games you can see in my games tab.
